Perhaps it was because of the horns but her divine smile seemed like a mask that concealed her true feelings. She wore a golden necklace that was patterned after a spiderweb which extended from her shoulders down to the tops of her breasts. Her slender wrists were covered in a pair of lustrous silk gloves and in her hand she held a strange weapon that looked like a wand of some sort. It was roughly 45 cm long and a black orb hovered at its end floating lightly in the air but holding its position at the end of the wand. Her name Momonga had not yet forgotten.
